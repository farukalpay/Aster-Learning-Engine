# Lighting Lab

Lighting Lab exercises direct light, ambient fill, contact shadows, emissive
material contribution, fog, and tone mapping under a stable camera.

Scene:

- `lighting-lab`: mixed roughness/metallic probes, warm emissive crystal, fogged receiver.

Preview:

```bash
./build/aster_preview --scene lighting-lab --output /tmp/aster_lighting_lab.ppm --width 1280 --height 720 --samples 2
```
